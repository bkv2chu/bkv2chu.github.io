Full-Color Single-Shot Holography
Victor Chu
Advised by Praneeth Chakravarthula, Felix Heide
Undergraduate Senior Thesis, 2023 (Outstanding Computer Science Senior Thesis Award)

In this work, we develop a novel method for full-color single-shot holography where we can reconstruct full-color holograms with only a single phase pattern. We then validate our results in simulation and experiment.
